🏢 About Apple Park

Apple Park is Apple's corporate headquarters, opened in 2017. This stunning 175-acre campus is home to over 12,000 employees and features the iconic ring-shaped main building, designed by Norman Foster. The campus is powered entirely by renewable energy and includes extensive green spaces and walking paths.
